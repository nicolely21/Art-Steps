For the third step, we will go into more detailed colouring. In my opinion, this is my favourite step because it’s colouring the image. You can choose any colour you want to colour your furniture and items. The same for this stage, you can use any brush you want and because it’s your own work and you can do it in the style you like best.

After colouring in my image, it looks like this.


I tried to go for similar colour themes, in this image, it means I coloured most stuff brown and green because they are the colour of nature in my opinion and so it’s not too colourful making it a little messy.

Tips/advice:

Picking a colour theme will set a different mood than just selecting any colours you want.
