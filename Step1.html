To start off, we will browse for an image for our reference. For beginners, you should choose images that you think are easy to draw like simple objects, plain rooms and stuff that has less patterns e.g., wallpapered walls. Drawing or tracing stuff could be hard if you choose an image related to nature because they have a lot of detail, especially in the leaves and water.


For this tutorial, I chose an image that is simple and doesn’t have much detailed stuff. What I would do first is paste the image into my drawing software. Make sure to put this in a new layer. I will name this layer ‘Reference’.


I will then change the opacity to make it less visual, on photoshop, to change the opacity of the layer, you click on the layer and find the opacity scale which would be above the below the layer tab. It will look like this.




Changing the opacity of the reference image will help when tracing he outline. For this step,
I would use a thin brush like felt tip small because it would make it look neater. After sketching/outlining
the image, you can add other items in or get rid of items in the sketch to make it look unique or to your
liking. It could look something like this.



A big change I made was instead of a clock on the wall, I added a shelf and instead of a tv that you can barely see on the reference image, I added a wardrobe.
