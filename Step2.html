The second step after you outline your sketch is to colour! This step is called base colouring and is where you colour the main areas of the sketch, for this example, the floor and the walls. You can choose the same colour as the reference image or you can choose your own colours. I chose to theme my drawing so it’s a nature theme. I added plants around the office sketch to set a nature look and for base colouring, I’m going to add a green wall. Here is what it will look like.




You can use any brush for this one because it just covers the walls and floor anyways. As you can see, I went over the lines and it’s a bit messy but that doesn’t matter because we’re going to cover it with the furniture layer anyways which will be on the next page.
